Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

NoBloat AI (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection

NoBloat AI collects and processes the following information only when voluntarily provided by the user:

  • Face photographs uploaded by the user for the Face Scan feature
  • Food photographs uploaded by the user for nutritional analysis
  • Daily water, sodium, and potassium intake data
  • Habit and routine completion data
  • Face scan analysis results, including numerical scores

We do not collect biometric identifiers, facial recognition data, facial geometry, Face ID data, ARKit face mesh data, TrueDepth camera data, or any form of identity verification data.

Additional optional data includes name and age (stored locally on-device for personalization) and onboarding survey responses (stored locally to customize recommendations). Anonymous app usage analytics may be collected to improve performance and user experience.

2. Face Data — Collection, Consent, and Use

When using the Face Scan feature, the user voluntarily provides a face photograph by taking a selfie or selecting an image from their photo library.

Before any face image is transmitted to a third-party AI service, the app displays a clear consent screen explaining how the image will be used and identifying the third-party service. The image is only transmitted after the user provides explicit consent.

The image is compressed to JPEG format and transmitted securely for AI-powered facial aesthetic analysis. It is used exclusively to:

  • Generate an AI-based facial analysis
  • Provide numerical ratings (overall score, jawline definition, cheekbone prominence, skin quality, masculinity, and potential)
  • Display personalized lifestyle recommendations
  • Allow the user to track facial debloating progress over time

Face data is never used for facial recognition, identification, authentication, advertising, profiling, or model training.

3. Face Data — Third-Party Sharing

Face photographs are transmitted solely to OpenAI via their GPT-4 Vision API through a secure server proxy for real-time processing. Face data is not sold, rented, or disclosed to advertisers, data brokers, or analytics providers. Face images are not stored on our servers. OpenAI processes the images according to its own privacy and data-handling policies and does not use submitted data to train AI models.

4. Face Data — Storage and Retention

Face photographs and their associated analysis results are stored securely on the user’s device only, using iOS app-protected local storage.

  • A maximum of 5 most recent face scans are retained locally
  • Older scans are automatically deleted when new scans exceed the limit
  • Face data is never uploaded to or stored in any cloud storage controlled by us
  • All locally stored face data is permanently deleted when the app is uninstalled or app data is cleared via device settings

5. Food Data

Food photographs are processed using the same safeguards as face data. They are sent securely to OpenAI for nutritional analysis, not stored on our servers, and stored locally on the user’s device only. Food images are not shared with any third party other than OpenAI for analysis purposes.

6. Data Security

All data transmitted for AI processing is sent over encrypted HTTPS connections using industry-standard TLS encryption. While we take reasonable measures to protect user data, no method of transmission is completely secure.

7. No Sale of Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise disclose personal data, face images, or food images for marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes.

8. Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI – Processes face and food images for AI analysis
  • Superwall – Manages subscription paywalls and may collect anonymous analytics
  • Apple Services – App Store and StoreKit for payments and subscriptions

All third-party services provide data protection consistent with applicable privacy standards.

9. Your Rights

  • Access all locally stored face data and analysis results in-app
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
  • Opt out of face scans at any time
  • Contact us for data inquiries at nobloatai@gmail.com

10. Children’s Privacy

NoBloat AI is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal or face data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted within the app and on this page.
