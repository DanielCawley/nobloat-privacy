Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 26, 2025

Introduction

Nobloat ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Photos and Images

  • Face Photos: When you use the face scanning feature, your photos are processed to analyze facial characteristics. These images are sent to our secure servers for AI analysis and are not permanently stored.
  • Food Photos: When you use the food scanning feature, your photos are processed to analyze nutritional content. These images are sent to our secure servers for AI analysis and are not permanently stored.

Usage Data

  • App Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data to improve the app experience, including feature usage and app performance metrics.
  • Progress Data: Your daily habits, streaks, and nutritional tracking data are stored locally on your device.

Information You Provide

  • Name and Age: If provided during onboarding, this information is stored locally on your device to personalize your experience.
  • Survey Responses: Your answers to onboarding questions are stored locally to customize recommendations.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide AI-powered face and food analysis
  • Track your debloating progress
  • Personalize recommendations and tips
  • Improve our app and services
  • Process in-app purchases

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

OpenAI

We use OpenAI's API to process face and food images for analysis. Images are sent securely and are processed according to OpenAI's privacy policy. Images are not used to train AI models.

Superwall

We use Superwall to manage our subscription paywall. Superwall may collect anonymous analytics data.

Apple Services

  • App Store: For processing subscriptions and payments
  • StoreKit: For managing in-app purchases

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Most of your personal data (progress, habits, preferences) is stored locally on your device
  • Secure Transmission: All data transmitted to our servers uses encrypted HTTPS connections
  • No Account Required: We do not require you to create an account, minimizing the personal data we collect
  • Image Processing: Photos sent for AI analysis are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers

Data Retention

  • Images submitted for analysis are processed immediately and not retained
  • Local data remains on your device until you delete the app
  • Subscription information is managed by Apple and subject to their retention policies

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored locally by the app
  • Delete: Remove all your data by deleting the app from your device
  • Opt-out: You can stop using features that require photo submission at any time

Children's Privacy

Nobloat is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

International Users

If you are accessing our app from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

Contact Us
